Chess Teaching Curriculum

1. The Chessboard & Setup

  • Understanding the 64 squares and coordinates
  • Proper piece arrangement

2. The Pieces & Their Moves

  • Pawn, Rook, Knight, Bishop, Queen, King
  • Special rules: castling, promotion, en passant

3. Core Rules

  • Check, checkmate, stalemate, draws

4. Opening Principles

  • Control of the center
  • Quick development
  • King safety

5. Basic Tactics & Endings

  • Simple checkmates (King + Queen vs. King)
  • Easy tactics like forks and pins

6. Practice Games

  • Mini games with limited pieces
  • Short matches with guidance
